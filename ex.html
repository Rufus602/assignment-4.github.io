About Us

Meyirzhan

Role: Head Chef

Meyirzhan is an experienced chef with a passion for creating exquisite dishes. He specializes in fusion cuisine and brings a unique blend of flavors to our restaurant. With his culinary expertise and attention to detail, Meyirzhan ensures that every dish that leaves the kitchen is of the highest quality.

Amirlan

Role: Restaurant Manager

Amirlan is a dedicated and skilled manager who oversees the day-to-day operations of our restaurant. With his strong leadership and organizational skills, he ensures that everything runs smoothly and efficiently. Amirlan is committed to providing excellent service to our customers and creating a welcoming atmosphere in our establishment.

Syrym

Role: Sommelier

Syrym is a certified sommelier with an extensive knowledge of wines and beverages. He curates our wine list and assists customers in selecting the perfect wine to complement their meals. Syrym's expertise and recommendations enhance the dining experience for our patrons, making their visit memorable and enjoyable.

Samal

Role: Pastry Chef

Samal is a talented pastry chef who creates delectable desserts that leave our customers craving for more. Her creativity and skill in pastry arts result in beautiful and mouth-watering treats. From decadent cakes to delicate pastries, Samal's desserts add a delightful touch to our menu and complete the dining experience.

Dariya

Role: Restaurant Host

Dariya is the friendly face that welcomes our guests and ensures they have a pleasant dining experience from the moment they step through the door. She assists with reservations, seating arrangements, and provides helpful recommendations. Dariya's warm and attentive nature sets the tone for excellent customer service in our restaurant.
